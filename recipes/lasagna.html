Lasagna

Description

Cook's Note:Make this recipe ahead of time and store in refrigerator. Also, freezes well for a healthy instant dinner on a busy night!

Nutrition Facts:Per Serving: 291 calories; protein 13.6g; carbohydrates 30.4g; fat 13.2g; cholesterol 39.1mg; sodium 481.9mg.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable broth, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons white wine
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup light ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups coarsely shredded carrots
  • 2 cups coarsely chopped zucchini
  • V1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
